FAQ
Questions people ask
before their first pickup.
Is there a minimum quantity needed for pickup?
No. We pick up from a single bundle of newspapers to a houseful of scrap. No minimums, no conditions. If you have scrap, we come.
How is the price decided?
Prices are based on Hyderabad market rates for the day. We weigh everything on a certified digital scale in front of you and calculate the total. You see every number in real time — nothing hidden.
How quickly can you come?
We offer same-day pickup across most of Hyderabad. Call before noon and we are usually there the same afternoon. Worst case, next morning.
Do I need to sort or prepare anything?
No. Our team sorts, separates, and carries everything themselves. All you need to do is show us where the scrap is. We handle the rest — you just collect your payment.
Is there any charge for the pickup service?
Zero charges. Pickup is completely free. We earn from recycling the materials — you get the scrap value in full with no deductions for transportation or labour.
Can I trust your team with home access?
Our staff are verified employees with ID cards and company uniforms. We confirm their name and photo before arrival. We have served 10,000+ homes across Hyderabad — safety and trust are non-negotiable for us.
