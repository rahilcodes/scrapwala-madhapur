Is there a minimum quantity required?
Not at all. We pick up from a single bundle of newspapers to a houseful of scrap. No minimums, no conditions — if you have it, we'll collect it.
How is the price decided?
Based on Hyderabad market rates for the day. We weigh everything on a certified digital scale right in front of you — you watch every number in real time. No hidden deductions.
How quickly can you arrive?
Same-day across most of Hyderabad. Call before noon and we are typically there the same afternoon. Earliest we've ever arrived was 45 minutes after a call.

No. Our team sorts, separates, and carries everything. Show us where the scrap is, and we handle the rest. You collect your payment and enjoy the free space.

Completely. We earn from recycling the materials — you receive the full scrap value with zero deductions for labour or transportation.

Our staff carry verified ID cards and wear company uniforms. We'll send you their name and photo before arrival. 10,000+ Hyderabad homes have trusted us — safety is non-negotiable for us.
