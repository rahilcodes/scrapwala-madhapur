Since 2018 in Hyderabad

We built Scrapwala because the scrap industry needed trust.

We're fundamentally changing how Hyderabad recycles by bringing absolute transparency, digital scales, and professional service to your doorstep.

The Problem

Most people avoid selling scrap because they simply do not trust the process.

Scrapwala started in 2018 when our founder noticed a simple problem: families in Hyderabad had scrap piling up at home — old newspapers, broken appliances, metal from renovations — but no reliable way to sell it. The local kabadiwala would show up unannounced, use a rigged scale, and pay whatever he felt like.

The Solution

We decided to fix the industry permanently through radical transparency.

We invested in NABL-certified digital scales, created a transparent pricing system based on daily market rates, trained a uniformed team with background checks, and introduced digital receipts on WhatsApp.
